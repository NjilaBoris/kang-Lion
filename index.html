▶ Kang Quintus

KQ

Kang Quintus is an award-winning Cameroonian filmmaker—director, actor, producer, and screenwriter—known for his immersive “method acting” style. As CEO of Kang Quintus Entertainment Ltd, he made history with The Fisherman’s Diary, the first Cameroonian film on Netflix and an Academy Awards submission, followed by Nganù in 2023. Beyond film, he holds an MBA in Accounting & Finance and is a Doctorate Fellow at the University of Maryland, blending storytelling with strategic expertise.
